MONKEY

Monkeys are intelligent, social animals known for their playful antics and dexterous hands. They live in troops and communicate using a variety of vocalizations, facial expressions, and gestures.

Monkeys are found in forests, savannahs, and mountains. Their prehensile tails and agile limbs make them expert climbers and swingers among the trees.
