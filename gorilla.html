ANIMALS MAP PLACES

GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest primates, known for their strength, intelligence, and gentle social bonds. They live in family groups led by a dominant silverback male.

Gorillas are mostly herbivorous, eating leaves, stems, and fruit. They communicate through vocalizations, gestures, and facial expressions, and are capable of using tools in the wild.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the gorillas at pen G18, in the rainforest habitat.
