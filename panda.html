PANDA

Giant pandas are beloved for their distinctive black-and-white fur and gentle nature. Native to China, pandas spend most of their day eating bamboo, which makes up 99% of their diet.

Pandas are excellent climbers and swimmers. Despite their size, they are shy and solitary animals, spending much of their time in dense bamboo forests.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM
